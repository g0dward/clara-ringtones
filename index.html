Ringtone playground

Twelve candidates to audition — the original set, riffs on the tones everyone already knows, and two built to cut through noise. Every tone is re-synthesised live from its notes, so you can tweak it in the browser: swap the instrument, drop or raise the octave, change the speed. Each ring alternates slightly so it never repeats mechanically. Land on something and hit Copy settings so we can render it to full quality.

Tip: to make e.g. "Beacon in the music-box voice, an octave down and slower" — open Beacon, set Instrument → musicbox, Octave → −1, Speed → 90%. Press play to hear it loop as it would when ringing. Best judged on a phone speaker, not a laptop.
